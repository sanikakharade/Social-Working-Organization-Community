About Us

A website that represents social issues and organizations working on these in a detailed manner. Includes issues such as deforestation, pollution, improper waste management. People are aware of social issues around them and are willing to help but are unaware of organizations working on these issues. This website tackles this problem by listing all the problems and organizations working for these issues.
